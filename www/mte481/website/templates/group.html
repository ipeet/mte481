Team Members

Iain Peet

Iain is Mechatronics engineering student who has done a great deal of work with robotics during school and work terms. He has been involved with the robotics team and the UW rocketry team designing software components. His previous work places have included Clearpath Robotics, Google, Sandvine, Virtual Button Technologies, Deluxe Laboratories. Iain will be providing the majority of the software for this project.

Jordan Valentin

Jordan is a student in the Mechatronics program. He has been the electrical lead for the midnight sun and formula hybrid teams at the University of Waterloo. His previous work terms have included hardware design at Nuvation and Research in Motion. He will be providing the majority of the hardware design and electrical support for this project.

Rowan Head-Marsden

Rowan is an engineer in the Mechatronics program at the University of Waterloo and has been part of a number of robotics team initiatives as a mechanical designer. For this project Rowan will be in charge of the mechanical aspects of the design, working with sensor mountings and mechanical integration.
